Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

Strawberry Street Events ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website strawberrystreetevents.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our Site or contact us.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form, including:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Organization or company name
  • Event type and details

We may also automatically collect certain technical data when you visit our Site, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited. This information is collected through standard web server logs and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and contact form submissions
  • Communicate with you about our services and your event needs
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is powered by Formspree, a third-party form processing service. When you submit the contact form, your information is transmitted through Formspree's servers. You can review Formspree's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

Our website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs including IP addresses. You can review Vercel's Privacy Policy for more information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our business (such as Formspree for form processing)
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Strawberry Street Events or others

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, including using HTTPS encryption on our website and security headers to prevent common web attacks. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

Our Site does not currently use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. If we add analytics or other cookie-based tools in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any future marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
